UwUic Gods

Fifty thousand years ago, a few sister angels known as Ionalns were wandering around the city of UwUs at night. They turned a wrong corner and entered the wrong tavern unintentionally, unaware of their mistake. While seated and reading, one of the sisters needed to use the bathroom. As she entered, she heard a loud scream from within. It turned out to be someone practicing dark magic. She hid quietly until they left, but when her sister arrived later, she noticed the person practicing dark magic and attempted to stop the attack. Unbeknownst to her, the attacker was a demon, and it stabbed her with its horns. The other sister managed to escape and sought help from the gods.


When the girl reached the gods, they inquired about her name. She introduced herself as Saysawnay and explained the situation, but she was out of breath and passed out before she could provide further details. The gods brought her to her home and left her there alone, disregarding the fact that she appeared to be around 17 years old. Saysawnay woke up to find the demon who had tried to kill her, Allnytishious, the god of UwUs, sitting beside her. Initially frightened, she soon realized the demon's identity. Unbeknownst to her, this encounter would become her worst fear and her only hope of finding happiness again.


As she opened her eyes fully, she noticed her injuries from the previous incident. Curious about when she had sustained them, she asked Allnytishious, even though she believed he wouldn't know. Surprisingly, he informed her that she had been asleep for five days. Realizing the urgency, she remembered her neighbor's imminent arrival to clean her home. Concerned about the potential consequences of the demon's presence in her neighborhood, she hurriedly led him to the treehouse in her backyard, instructing him to hide there until she returned. What she did during that time remains unknown, but she returned after approximately 20 minutes, badly injured.


Confused, the demon asked what had happened, but before she could respond, she passed out from her wounds. Aware that he needed to kill her before she regained her memory, the demon prepared to strike. However, a mysterious figure suddenly appeared and warned him against it, threatening to kill him if he proceeded. Ignoring the warning, the demon attempted to cut Saysawnay's arm with a knife, but the stranger shot lightning at him, injuring his chest. The stranger then took her away to safety. Though it seemed like the stranger had vanished, he had simply run an incredible distance in a matter of seconds and lay on a nearby table.


When Saysawnay regained consciousness, she found herself surrounded by five men in suits. Unsure of her whereabouts, she instinctively attacked, resulting in a brief fight. The stranger intervened and convinced them to cease hostilities. Saysawnay, realizing they would have killed her already if they intended harm, stopped fighting. The stranger instructed her to sit down and began explaining the situation. She learned that she was not meant to be in Avanlot; it was a mistake made by the gods. They wanted to send her back to Earth, but she refused, expressing her unwillingness to leave. The stranger then offered her a choice: learn magic or face death.


Deciding to learn magic, Saysawnay expressed her desire to know the stranger's name. He refused to disclose it and told her to call him Anark. When she asked about their current location, he replied, "the Kingdom of Crabtoes," disregarding the strange name. Saysawnay agreed to follow Anark and left with him, stepping outside to find out why she had been brought there. She began her journey to find the demon, not with the intention to kill him, but to befriend him and use his help in defeating a greater evil, thereby saving the world. However, her path was filled with menacing creatures as she traversed the streets. She considered checking the caves and mountains, hoping to locate the demon.


Upon reaching the first cave, she discovered the demon near death. Sympathetic, she offered her assistance without understanding the risk. To her surprise, the demon pulled out a knife and accused her of trying to kill him as well. Confused, she inquired about his accusation, and he admitted that Anark had been attempting to kill him since he was 14 years old. The demon's accidental spell almost killed Anark, resulting in their ongoing conflict. Saysawnay asked if the demon had intended to harm her sister, to which he replied that it wasn't intentional. However, he stated that he had to defend himself, as her sister would have killed him. The demon apologized, expressing remorse for his actions. Although Saysawnay comprehended his explanation, she was still curious why he hadn't simply asked for blood. He then requested her blood, explaining his longing for it. Saysawnay agreed, understanding his situation due to her past experiences with her vampire brother. After the demon had taken her blood, Anark entered the scene, prepared to kill him. However, Saysawnay intervened, stating that the demon's cooperation was necessary for their mission. Reluctantly, Anark agreed but set certain conditions, including no more blood and the demon not staying at his home.


The demon accepted the conditions, and Saysawnay accompanied him to his mansion. Inside, she marveled at the grandeur and intricate details. However, her curiosity led her to stumble upon a box, which turned out to contain a gruesome severed head. Terrified, she screamed, alerting the demon. He grabbed her and locked both of them in a room. With a knife in hand, he began performing blood magic to manipulate her mind, taking advantage of her vulnerable state.


In the dimly lit room, the demon's eyes glowed with an unsettling intensity as he delved into his dark magic. Saysawnay struggled against his grip, her heart pounding with fear and desperation. She pleaded with him to stop, her voice trembling with uncertainty.


"Please, don't do this," she begged, her voice filled with anguish. "I thought we were on the same side, working towards a common goal. Why would you betray me like this?"


The demon's expression hardened, his features contorted with a mix of anger and determination. "You don't understand," he growled, his voice laced with frustration. "I need to know your true intentions. This is the only way to ensure that you won't betray us."


Summoning her inner strength, Saysawnay focused her thoughts and tapped into her own latent magical abilities. With a surge of energy, she pushed against the demon's influence, creating a psychic barrier between her mind and his dark magic.


As the demon continued to chant and gesture, his frustration grew, sensing the resistance from Saysawnay. His attempts to break through her mental defenses became more aggressive, his magic lashing out like tendrils of darkness.


But Saysawnay refused to yield. She called upon the training she had received from Anark, focusing on her connection to the elemental forces. With a surge of power, she unleashed a wave of radiant energy, pushing back against the demon's dark magic.


The room quivered with the clash of opposing forces, crackling with energy as their powers clashed. The demon staggered, momentarily caught off guard by the strength of Saysawnay's resistance.


Seeing an opportunity, she seized it. Saysawnay mustered her strength and delivered a swift kick to the demon's chest, sending him sprawling backward. Gasping for breath, she sprinted towards the door, her heart pounding with a mixture of fear and determination.


As she reached the door, it swung open, and Anark stood there, his eyes widening in surprise as he took in the scene. Before he could react, Saysawnay rushed past him, tears streaming down her face.


Anark quickly regained his composure and chased after her, his voice filled with concern. "Saysawnay, wait! Please, let me explain!"


But she didn't stop. She ran through the dimly lit corridors of the mansion, her mind consumed with a mix of fear, anger, and confusion. She needed time to process what had just transpired, to make sense of the demon's betrayal and her own newfound powers.


With every step, the world around her blurred, her surroundings becoming a mere backdrop to the turmoil within her mind. She didn't know where she was going or what lay ahead, but one thing was certain – her journey had taken an unexpected turn, and she had to find her own path through the darkness, both within and without.



As Saysawnay raced through the twisting corridors of the mansion, she could hear Anark's footsteps echoing behind her. She couldn't bring herself to face him just yet, not after what she had witnessed and experienced. She needed time to collect her thoughts and understand the truth behind the demon's actions.

Finally, she found herself in a secluded room, away from prying eyes and the chaotic energy that permeated the mansion. She collapsed onto a plush chair, her body trembling with a mix of exhaustion and adrenaline. Her mind was a whirlwind of conflicting emotions and unanswered questions.


Gazing out of the room's ornate window, Saysawnay took in the night sky, searching for a moment of solace and clarity. The stars twinkled above, casting a gentle glow on her tear-stained face. She tried to steady her breathing, seeking inner peace amidst the chaos that surrounded her.


Lost in her thoughts, she replayed the events in her mind, dissecting each interaction, each word spoken. There had to be more to the demon's story, something that explained his desperate attempt to gain control over her mind. She couldn't ignore the fleeting moments of vulnerability she had glimpsed in his eyes.


Deep down, Saysawnay couldn't completely dismiss the connection she felt with the demon, despite the betrayal. The brief encounters and shared experiences had sparked something within her, a sense of understanding that transcended the boundaries of their roles as enemies.


But trust had been shattered, and the wounds of her sister's death still fresh in her heart. Saysawnay knew she couldn't allow herself to be swayed by sympathy or misplaced compassion. Her duty was to protect the world from the malevolent forces that threatened its existence.


As the first rays of dawn painted the sky with hues of pink and gold, a newfound determination ignited within Saysawnay's soul. She would confront the demon once more, seeking answers and closure. She had to understand his motives and decide whether he could be an ally or a formidable enemy.


With resolve in her eyes, Saysawnay rose from the chair and made her way back through the winding halls of the mansion. Anark awaited her outside the room, concern etched across his face. She held up a hand, silently signaling for him to give her space.


Anark nodded, respecting her need for solitude. "When you're ready, we'll talk," he said softly, his voice filled with a mix of regret and understanding.


Saysawnay acknowledged his words with a nod before venturing further into the mansion, her heart heavy with the weight of her choices. The path ahead was uncertain, but she knew that only by delving deeper into the shadows could she uncover the truth and find her own place within this complex world of magic and demons.


With each step, Saysawnay steeled herself for the challenges to come, determined to navigate the treacherous landscape and discover her role in the destiny that awaited her.


As Saysawnay continued her journey through the sprawling mansion, she couldn't shake off the memories of her encounters with the demon. Despite the initial betrayal, there was a lingering connection that seemed to defy reason. Their shared moments had sparked something within her, something that resembled compassion and understanding.


In the midst of her reflections, she stumbled upon a hidden chamber, concealed behind a grand tapestry. Inside, she found ancient tomes and scrolls, revealing forgotten prophecies and the secrets of the demon king. It became clear that the demon king's reign of terror was reaching its peak, and a battle for the fate of their world was imminent.


Saysawnay's heart swelled with determination as she realized the path that lay before her. She knew that her destiny was intertwined with the demon she had come to care for, and together they would challenge the demon king's tyranny.


With newfound purpose, Saysawnay sought out the demon, finding him deep within the mansion's labyrinthine corridors. As she approached, he turned to face her, his eyes filled with a mix of anguish and longing.


"We have a choice to make," Saysawnay began, her voice filled with resolve. "We can either let the world crumble under the demon king's rule, or we can stand together and fight for the future we believe in."


The demon's gaze softened, and he took a step closer to her. "I never intended for our paths to cross like this," he confessed. "But the more time I spent with you, the more I realized there is goodness in this world worth protecting."


Their eyes locked, a silent understanding passing between them. Love had bloomed amidst chaos, and together they would defy the odds and challenge the very foundation of their existence.

Gathering allies and harnessing their combined strengths, Saysawnay and the demon prepared for the final showdown with the demon king. They trained tirelessly, honing their magic and strategizing for the battle ahead. Their connection grew stronger with each passing day, intertwining their destinies and solidifying their shared purpose.


However, before they could confront the demon king, news reached them that a powerful warrior had already managed to defeat him. The victory was bittersweet, for while the immediate threat had been vanquished, a new power vacuum threatened to engulf their world in chaos.


Undeterred, Saysawnay and the demon knew that their journey was far from over. They set out to find the warrior who had defeated the demon king, seeking their guidance and assistance. Together, they formed an unlikely alliance, united by the common goal of bringing stability and peace to their land.


As they embarked on their quest, facing treacherous landscapes and formidable foes, Saysawnay and the demon grew closer, their love and trust fueling their determination. They discovered hidden truths, ancient relics, and unleashed powers within themselves that surpassed their wildest imaginations.


In the face of adversity, they fought side by side, their powers intertwining in a harmonious dance. Together, they overcame obstacles and encountered dark creatures that threatened to tear them apart. But their love remained unyielding, a beacon of hope amidst the encroaching darkness.


Ultimately, the day of reckoning arrived. Saysawnay, the demon, and their newfound allies confronted the true source of evil, a malevolent force that sought to plunge their world into eternal darkness. With the weight of their shared destiny, they fought with unwavering determination and unwavering love.


In a climactic battle, Saysawnay and the demon unleashed their combined powers, their love amplifying their magic. As their spells clashed with the malevolent force, a brilliant light erupted, shattering the darkness and restoring balance to their world.


In the aftermath of that battle.


On Earth, Saysawnay and the demon, whose bond had deepened over time, found themselves sentenced to live among humans due to their incredible strength and magical abilities. They were forced to conceal their true identities and keep their magic a secret. They obtained a humble home where they carried out mundane chores while secretly embarking on thrilling adventures.


As they explored their newfound life on Earth, Saysawnay and the demon realized that their unique abilities could be utilized for a greater purpose. They decided to establish an organization dedicated to protecting the world from magical threats and maintaining balance. This organization, known as Dalanoko, grew steadily as more individuals with hidden magical talents joined their cause.


Meanwhile, in the city of UwUs, the most powerful god, driven by his own desires, betrayed his own planet and seized control. He became a tyrant, ruling with an iron fist, causing chaos and suffering. However, the people of UwUs remained unaware of the events unfolding on Earth.


Back on Earth, Dalanoko continued to expand, gathering a diverse group of individuals with powers, known as Gonotos. The organization trained them to use their abilities responsibly and to defend humanity against those who sought to exploit their powers for personal gain. Together, they fought against the rising threat of the humans with powers who had embraced their abilities for destructive purposes.


Unfortunately, despite Dalanoko's efforts, the humans with powers grew in number and influence. Driven by their newfound abilities, they began to wreak havoc upon the world, causing widespread destruction and endangering innocent lives. Dalanoko, now facing a grave challenge, had to take drastic measures to protect the planet.


Realizing the urgency of the situation, Dalanoko made the difficult decision to recruit some of the individuals with powers who had turned to chaos. They offered them a chance at redemption, urging them to join forces and eliminate the common enemy that threatened their existence. Together, they formed an uneasy alliance, vowing to bring an end to the chaos and restore balance.


Their united efforts led to intense battles against the rogue individuals with powers who aimed to destroy the world. The clashes were fierce and destructive, testing the resolve and strength of both sides. Gradually, Dalanoko and their unlikely allies gained the upper hand, defeating the renegades and neutralizing the immediate threat.


However, their victory came at a steep price. The humans who had once revered Dalanoko as protectors now despised them for their differences and the chaos that had unfolded. They turned against Dalanoko and the individuals with powers, branding them as outcasts and threats to their way of life.


Undeterred by the animosity, Dalanoko persisted, realizing the importance of educating the general population about magic and the responsible use of powers. They worked tirelessly to foster understanding and cooperation between humans and individuals with powers, seeking to mend the fractured society and rebuild trust.


As their organization continued its efforts to unite and uplift humanity, a cataclysmic event occurred. The all-powerful god from the city of UwUs unleashed a devastating surge of magic upon Earth. Half of the human population was imbued with incredible powers, much like the individuals Dalanoko had recruit.

This unexpected turn of events caused chaos and confusion on a global scale. While Dalanoko tried to maintain order and guide the newly empowered individuals, many succumbed to the allure of their newfound abilities. They formed their own groups, each with their own agendas, further destabilizing the world

Amidst the turmoil, Dalanoko found themselves confronted with a monumental challenge. They had to quell the unrest, prevent further destruction, and ensure the survival of humanity. With the support of the individuals they had recruited, Dalanoko redoubled their efforts, striving to restore harmony and protect Earth from its own self-destruction.


Facing opposition from both the humans and the newly empowered individuals, Dalanoko's path was fraught with obstacles. Nevertheless, they remained determined to fulfill their mission, fostering unity, and working towards a future where humans and those with powers could coexist in harmony. The road ahead was arduous, but the members of Dalanoko remained unwavering in their resolve to safeguard the world they had come to call home.

Saysawnay's mind raced, searching for a way to break free from his grasp. She knew that if she allowed the demon to manipulate her mind, he would gain control over her thoughts and memories, potentially turning her against her companions and jeopardizing their mission. She had to find a way to resist his magic.

-Zeal Kimone
